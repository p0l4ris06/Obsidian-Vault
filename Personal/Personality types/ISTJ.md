## Positive traits
- Dependable, trustworthy
- Highly intelligent
- Mentally organised
- Loyal
- Responsible
- Good listeners
- Tendency to be perfectionists
-  Caring and protective over those they love

## Negative traits
- Can be know-it-alls
- Tendency to be extremely judgemental
- Can be cheap/stingy
- Not naturally in tune with others emotions
